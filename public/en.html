  • About the Company

    Credlin Co., Ltd was established in 1994 to develop and manufacture specialty water-based polymers. The pilot plant was set up in 1996 in Youth Industrial Park in Taichung, Taiwan to start the small scale production of pigment printing thickeners. The main building was completed in 2000 for large scale productions with the capacity of 500 metric tons per month.

  • Our Approach

    The factory is fully equipped with environment friendly devices with stringent air and waste pollution control. Supported by extensive research and development capabilities, we offer a wide range of synthetic thickeners especially for textile pigment printing industry and cosmetic applications, also many acrylic latexes for different applications such as construction waterproof materials, coatings and adhesives.

  • Quality Service

    We value product quality and customer service. By supporting, communicating and working together with our customers, we will grow together and make a difference.

底塗樹脂 AP-300

底塗樹脂 AP-300 是一種壓克力共聚合的水性樹脂，因具有微細粒子及特殊分子結構，可以深入水泥內部，形成網狀結構，填滿毛細孔，增進底材的強度，改進上層水泥砂漿的施工性，並且做為新舊水泥之間的橋樑，提昇接著強度，效果非常好。


本產品具有下列特點：

  1. 純壓克力系高分子聚合物，耐候性與耐水性都很好，適合室內室外使用。
  2. 對底材的接著力強，薄層光亮，潑水性很好。
  3. 施工容易，可用噴、塗、或刷的方法施工，清洗容易，可 DIY 施工。
  4. 水性樹脂，沒有溶劑污染與臭味。
  5. 表面可做自平泥、上漆，貼磁磚，或其它任何後施工。

規格

主要成份壓克力共聚合物
外觀乳白色半透明乳液
固成份49.0 ±1.0%
pH8.0 ± 1.0
比重1.06
黏度9,000 ± 3,000 cps (Brookfield, RVT, #6, 5 rpm)
Tg15℃

使用範例

先將地坪表面所有油脂、灰塵污垢或鬆動之混凝土清除乾淨，第一遍先將底塗樹脂AP-300 與四倍的水稀釋後，噴塗或刷在水泥面上，約30分鐘後再將底塗樹脂 AP-300 與兩倍的水稀釋後做第二遍施工，底塗樹脂 AP-300 可滲入毛細孔，在約半小時內形成一層牢固的防水保護膜，此時即可澆注水泥砂漿、自平泥、彈泥等後施工。 使用量視地面狀況而定，每公斤約可塗佈 5 到 7 平方米。

*以上僅供參考， 請依實際需求做調整。

ACRYLIC PRIMER AP-300

Acrylic Primer AP-300 is an acrylic emulsion copolymer with very fine particle sizes and good adhesion to many different substrates. It can penetrate into the substrates to form a strong network, provide high level of water resistance, improve the adhesion between old and new concretes, and help the workability for self-leveling concretes.


This product has the following benefits:

  1. Excellent water and abrasion resistance.
  2. Good adhesion to the substrates.
  3. Good workability.
  4. Environmentally friendly.
  5. Suitable for any construction work.

Typical Physical Properties

AppearanceLight translucent emulsion
Solids49.0 ± 1.0%
pH8.0 ± 1.0
Specific gravity1.06
Viscosity9,000 ± 3,000 cps (Brookfield, RVT, #6, 5 rpm)
Tg15℃

Method of Use

Remove all loose material and contaminates from substrate. Apply a thin uniform coating of Acrylic Primer AP-300 (diluted with 4X volumes of water) using either a spray or a brush. About thirty minutes later, apply the second coating of Acrylic Primer AP-300 (diluted with 2X volumes of water). Allow Primer to dry and make sure the ground is tack free (about 30 minutes), then apply self-leveling underlayment, concrete, elastic cement or any other construction works. The coverage of Acrylic Primer AP-300 will depend on surface porosity; usually 1Kg AP-300 can cover about 5 to 7 square meters.

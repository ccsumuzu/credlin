合成糊劑(免用油增稠劑) AT-73

增稠劑 AT-73 是一種高吸水性壓克力共聚合樹脂，適用於所有水性樹脂產品之增稠，因含有特殊原料，提昇增稠效果及改變流動性，本產品所調製之印花漿特別適合平板式或滾筒式印花機使用。增稠劑 AT-73 可直接加入所欲增稠的系統中使用或先與水混合增稠後再與樹脂混合，不需高速攪拌。


增稠劑 AT-73 具有下列特點：

  1. 非常容易使用，迅速增稠。
  2. 增稠效果良非常好，施工性佳。
  3. 耐電解質，不滲邊。
  4. 顯色均勻、鮮豔。
  5. 適用範圍廣。

規格

外觀淡黃色乳液
pH8.0 ±1.0 (微具氨水味道)
固成份32.0 ±1.0%
比重1.20
黏度25,000 ± 5,000 cps (Brookfield, RVT, #6, 5 rpm)

使用方法

水與適當比例之增稠劑 AT-73 先混合增稠，再加入印花樹脂、顏料和其它添加物，攪拌均勻即可。

參考應用例

水(w/w%)93
增稠劑(w/w%)3.0
混和均勻，黏度約80,000 - 100,000 cps (Brookfield, RVT, #6, 5 rpm)，再加
印花樹脂(w/w%)3.0
顏料（藍色）(w/w%)1.0
印花漿黏度約35,000 - 45,000 cps (Brookfield, RVT, #6, 5 rpm)
以上四種原料一起混和，攪拌均勻亦可，顯色和施工性都很好。
本例僅供參考，請依實際需求做調整。黏度會因水質和其它添加物而有些微變化。

THICKENER AT-73

Thickener AT-73 is a synthetic acrylic copolymer dispersed in a paraffin solvent and is developed for water based polymer products. This product provides high thickening efficiency, short flow rheology, and very good mechanical stability, suitable for textile pigment printing industry, especially for the rotary screen-printing. Because of its special molecular structure, it has a very high thickening efficiency and a unique rheology. The product can be used directly and does not require the addition of ammonia or other alkali to the system.


This product has the following benefits:

  1. Extremely easy to use and highly efficient.
  2. Excellent workability, short flow and spatter resistant.
  3. High electrolyte resistance and high flush resistance.
  4. Superior definition and color yield.
  5. Effective over a wide pH range.

Typical Physical Properties

AppearanceCreamy liquid
pH8.0 ±1.0 (light ammonia odor)
Solids32.0 ±1.0%
Specific gravity1.20
Viscosity25,000 ± 5,000 cps (Brookfield, RVT, #6, 5 rpm)

Method of Use and Example

Water is added to the pre-weighed Thickener AT-73 with gentle agitation to prepare a thickener stock. Binder, pigment, and other ingredients of the printing paste formulation are then added to the thickener stock to make the final printing paste. For example, a printing paste with the composition below has a viscosity of 35,000 - 45,000 cps (Brookfield, RVT, #6, 5 rpm), which gives excellent print definition and color yield.

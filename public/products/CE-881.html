彈性水泥樹脂 CE-881

彈性水泥樹脂 CE-881 是特別針對防水用彈性水泥而研製的水性壓克力/苯乙烯共聚合樹脂。


本產品具有下列特點：

  1. 壓克力系高分子聚合物，耐候性與耐水性都很好，適合室內室外防水用。
  2. 與水泥完全相容，對各種不同的底材，比如舊水泥、磚、木材、金屬、PVC、保利龍等都有很好的接著力。
  3. 施工容易，可用噴、塗、或刷的方法施工，清洗容易，可 DIY 施工。
  4. 水性樹脂，沒有溶劑污染與臭味，底材表面潮濕亦可施工。
  5. 施工後之薄層柔軟強韌，可曲折不龜裂，防水效果極佳。
  6. 表面可上漆，貼磁磚，或其它任何後施工。
  7. 可局部施工，不必像 PU 需全面施工，單位面積價格也比 PU 便宜。

規格

主要成份壓克力共聚合物
外觀乳白色乳液
固成份49.0 ± 1.0%
pH8.5 ± 1.0
比重約 1.06
黏度25,000 ±10,000 cps (Brookfield RVT #6, 5 rpm)
Tg約 -5℃

使用範例

地坪表面所有油脂、灰塵污垢或鬆動之混凝土需先清除乾淨。水泥或預拌骨材與 CE-881 乳液以約 1 比 1 (重量比) 混合，攪拌均勻即可使用，簡單方便，1 至 2 毫米的厚度就有極佳的防水效果。 每平方公尺約需 1公斤 CE-881 彈性水泥樹脂。如使用在垂直面，可酌量添加少量海菜粉或增稠劑，以防垂流。

ELASTIC CEMENT LATEX CE-881

Elastic cement latex CE-881 is an acrylic-styrene emulsion copolymer with special protecting system and is compatible with cement and many other types of filler. It is designed as the waterproof product for construction application by mixing one part of CE-881 and one part of cement-based powder. The resulting mixture will form a waterproofing membrane with excellent water resistance and elasticity when it is applied on the substrate and air-dried. The product is an environmentally friendly aqueous dispersion suitable for use in new construction, restoration of old construction, indoor and outdoor.


This product has the following benefits:

  1. Excellent water resistance suitable for outdoor and indoor usage.
  2. Unequalled bond strength to a wide variety of substrates such as old cement, brickwork, wood, and metal.
  3. Excellent workability.
  4. Can be worked on a wet surface.
  5. Environmental friendly.
  6. Superior mechanical (tensile strength and elongation) properties.
  7. Suitable for painting, tiling or other construction work.
  8. Not like PU, it can be partially worked on the restoration area.

Method of Use

Prepare a good, clean surface at all times. Remove all loose, disintegrated material, dirt grease, oil, and efflorescence. Substrate must be structurally sound. A mortar can be prepared by thoroughly mixing equal weight of cement-based powder and CE-881 before application. It is easy to use and the coverage of CE-881 will depend on surface porosity. Usually, 1kg CE-881 can cover about one square meter at about 1mm dry film thickness and give excellent waterproof performance. If apply on a vertical surface, the addition of about 0.5% thickener is recommended for a better workability.

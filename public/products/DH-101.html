水性壓克力樹脂 DH-101

水性壓克力樹脂 DH-101 是壓克力與苯乙烯共聚合的水性樹脂，經由特殊聚合製程，產生特殊的分子結構和粒子形態，降低成膜溫度，在常溫下，不必使用揮發性有機溶劑的輔助，即可形成同時具有柔軟、強韌、低回黏性、高伸展性、高光澤、優良接著力和優良耐水性的保護膜，主要應用於木材塗料。本產品對各種添加劑也具有優良的接納能力，可因需求去做成品的調製。

規格

主要成份壓克力苯乙烯共聚合物，陰離子
外觀乳白色半透明乳液
固成份38.0 ± 1.0%
pH8.0 ± 1.0
粒徑~80 nm
黏度12,000 ± 3,000 cps (RVT, #6, 10rpm)
成膜溫度約 15℃

WATER-BASED ACRYLIC LATEX DH-101

Water-based acrylic latex DH-101 is an acrylic-styrene emulsion polymer with special molecular structures and particle properties manufactured through a unique process to lower the film-forming temperature. Without the aid of volatile solvents, waterproofing films with excellent adhesion, durability, film flexibility, strength, and high gloss can be formed at ambient temperatures,mainly applied as coatings for wooden materials. This product is exceptionally susceptible to a wide variety of additives, and can be easily formulated with other ingredients.


Typical Physical Properties

AppearanceLight translucent emulsion
Solids38.0 ± 1.0%
pH8.0 ± 1.0
Diameter~80 nm
Viscosity12,000 ± 3,000 cps (Brookfield, RVT, #6, 10 rpm)
TgAbout 15℃

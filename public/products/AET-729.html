增稠劑 AET-729

增稠劑 AET-729 是一種鹼膨脹締結型壓克力共聚合樹脂，適用於所有水性樹脂產品之增稠，因為特殊之分子結構，增稠效果非常好，具有低剪切高稠度，高剪切低稠度，施工容易，防垂流的特性。本產品可直接加入所欲增稠的系統中混合均勻後，再調整酸鹼值到 pH 8.0-9.0 之間，即可得到最佳增稠效果。


本產品具有下列特點：

  1. 本身黏度很低，非常容易使用。
  2. 遇鹼迅速增稠，效果很好。
  3. 增稠後，乳液具有低剪切高稠度，高剪切低稠度之特性。
  4. 增稠後，用酸調整到 pH 6.0 左右仍具增稠效果。
  5. 適用範圍廣，具長期稠度安定性。

規格

外觀淡白色乳液
固成份29.5 ±1.0%
pH3.0 ±0.5
比重1.05
黏度< 100 cps (Brookfield, RVT, #1, 5 rpm)

參考應用例

增稠劑(w/w%)4.0
水(w/w%)91.0
NaOH (1%) 水溶液21.0
黏度25,000 (#6, 10 rpm)
本例僅供參考，請依實際需求做調整。

THICKENER AET-729

Thickener AET-729 is an alkali swellable associative acrylic thickener and thickens instantly when neutralized to a pH between 8.0 and 9.0. Because of its special molecular structure, it has a very high thickening efficiency, a unique rheology, and the viscosity will not drop at lower pH (about 6.0). The product is provided in latex form with low viscosity and is suitable for most of water-based products.


This product has the following benefits:

  1. Extremely easy to use and highly efficient.
  2. Excellent workability for most of applications.
  3. High electrolyte resistance.
  4. Short flow, very high viscosity at low shear.
  5. Long term viscosity stability.

Typical Physical Properties

AppearanceWhite emulsion
pH3.0 ± 0.5
Solids29.5 ±1.0%
Specific gravity1.05
Viscosity< 100 cps (Brookfield, RVT, #1, 5 rpm)
Thickened ground water*20,000 ± 5,000 cps (Brookfield, RVT, #6, 10rpm)
*NaOH solution (1%, 21g) is added to a mixture of ground water (91g) and AET-729 (4g).

Method of Use and Example

If the system to be thickened has a pH below 5, AET-729 can be added directly with gentle agitation followed by the addition of an alkali solution to adjust the pH to between 8 and 9 for the best thickening efficiency. On the other hand, if the system to be thickened has a higher pH, it is recommended to dilute AET-729 with at least same amount of water for a better dispersion. An alkali solution is then added to adjust the pH to between 8 and 9. Application areas include any latex polymers, detergents, defoamers, cosmetic products, etc.

面塗樹脂 M-349

面塗樹脂 M-349 是針對耐磨塗料而研製的水性壓克力共聚合樹脂，配合特殊原料組合及先進的製程，本產品具有下列特點：

  1. 與各種粉體填充料相容性良好，可調製單液型耐磨塗料成品。
  2. 施工容易，清洗容易，可 DIY 施工。
  3. 水性樹脂，沒有溶劑污染與臭味，底材表面潮濕亦可施工。
  4. 施工自然乾燥後之薄層堅硬強韌，可耐一定程度之磨損。

規格

主要成份壓克力苯乙烯共聚合物，陰離子
外觀乳白色乳液
固成份41.0 ± 1.5%
pH8.0 ± 1.0
粒徑~ 100 nm
黏度2,500 ± 1000 cps (Brookfield, RVT #3, 10 rpm)
Tg約 40℃ (計算值)

使用範例

本產品可添加約等重量之粉體填充料，如鈦白粉、碳酸鈣、或各種顏料，相容性良好，所調製之單液型塗料成品，施工容易且具優良接著力及耐磨功能，客戶請依個別需求做配比調整。

COATING LATEX M-349

Coating Latex M-349 is water-based acrylic copolymer that was designed for abrasion and wear resistant coatings. Formulated with special ingredients and manufactured with our unique process, this product has the following benefits:

  1. Excellent capability with various powder filling materials.
  2. Excellent workability, easy to apply and clean.
  3. Water-based without solvent pollution, can be used on wet surfaces.
  4. Forms a thin, anti-abrasive film with high strength.

Typical Physical Properties

AppearanceWhite emulsion
Solids41.0 ± 1.5%
pH8.0 ± 1.0
Diameter~ 100 nm
Viscosity 2,500 ± 1000 cps (Brookfield, RVT, #3, 10 rpm)
TgAbout 40℃

Method of Use

Coating Latex M-349 can be formulated with equal weight of powder filling materials, such as titanium dioxide, calcium carbonate, or pigments, with great capability. The formulated coating material is easy to apply on surfaces and provides exceptional adhesion and anti-abrasive properties. The formulation should be adjusted to the product requirement.
